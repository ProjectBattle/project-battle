About the park

A straightforward place to stay for longer visits

Aviator Cove RV Park is a practical option for guests who need a dependable place to stay for weeks or months at a time. The park is located in Newport, Arkansas, near the Newport airport and runways, with a calm setting that suits routine and work schedules.

We welcome long-term guests who value a simple setup, clear expectations, and a quiet place to settle in.

Monthly stay information

Monthly stays are welcome when availability allows

Monthly stays are welcome for guests with ongoing plans. Rent is due on the 1st of each month, and availability is limited.

All guests must be approved before moving in. If you are planning a longer stay in Newport, Arkansas, please reach out early so we can discuss current availability.

Important notes

Before you apply, please review the basics

  • Pets require approval before move-in.
  • Not all sites are drive-through.
  • Availability changes often.
  • Monthly rent is not prorated or refunded once paid unless approved by management.
  • Guests are responsible for keeping their site clean and respectful.
